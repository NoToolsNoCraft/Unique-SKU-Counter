Unique SKU Counter

Quickly find how many unique SKU codes you have in your product list.

Why Use This Tool?

Managing large product lists is often challenging, especially when your data contains multiple duplicate SKUs. For example, if a product code is accidentally listed several times in inventory or order reports, it can cause inaccurate stock counts, ordering mistakes, and confusion across teams.

This tool quickly identifies and counts the unique SKUs from any list you paste in — saving you time, improving accuracy, and helping you maintain clean, reliable product data for inventory management, sales reporting, or e-commerce catalogs.

How to Use

  1. Copy your list of SKUs from any source (spreadsheet, inventory system, email, etc.).
  2. Paste the entire list into the text area below.
  3. Click "Count Unique SKUs".
  4. View the total count and the unique SKU codes displayed in columns.

By quickly spotting duplicates and knowing exactly which SKUs are unique, you can reduce errors and streamline your workflows.
